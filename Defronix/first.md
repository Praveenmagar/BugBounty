# Steps to success in Bug Bounty
  - **Keep learning**
  - **Be a Learner, Earning will come**
  - **Focus on Concept**

# Choosing right target
  - **Scope- Large Vs Small**
  - **Publiv VS Private**
  - **Rewards/Bounty**
  - **Triage Team/Company**
  - **According to your skill**
  - **Web app/Mobile App/Others**
  - **Using Assetfinder**
  - **Using google dork**
## For example
  **Target A**
```text
abc.com
```
- 5000 Researchers, Less scope, Triage(Response) time much more, according to your skill

  **Target B**
```text
xyz.com
```
- 1000 Researchers, big scope, less triage time, no skills of yours

***Here, abc.com is best to hunt**
