# Red Team Threat Intelligence

- Threat Intelligence (TI) or Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) is the information, or TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures), attributed to an adversary(attacker red team trying to imitate), commonly used by defenders to aid in detection measures.
- The red cell can leverage CTI from an offensive perspective to assist in adversary emulation.

- **What is Threat Intelligence?**
    -  From a red team perspective, you can think of threat intelligence as the red team's analysis of the blue team's ability to properly leverage CTI for detections.
    - To aid in consuming CTI(Cyber Threat Intelligence) and collecting TTPs, red teams will often use threat intelligence platforms and frameworks such as MITRE ATT&CK, TIBER-EU, and OST Map.
    - These cyber frameworks will collect known TTPs and categorize them based on varying characteristics such as,
        - Threat Group
        - Kill Chain Phase
        - Tactic
        - Objective/Goal
