## Manipulation Vs Inspiration
- Most companies have no clue why their customers are their customers
- They even don't know why their employees are their employees either
- There are two ways to influence human behaviour
    1. You can manipulate it
    2. you can inspire it

    - Manipulate ways
        - Price
        - Promotions
            - In business to business world, promotions are valled "value added"
        - Fear
        - Aspirations(Strong desire)
            - Fear motivates us to move away from something horrible while aspirations tempt us toward something desirable.
            - Some examples
                - Six steps to a happier life
                - Work those abs to your dream dress size
                - In six short weeks you can be rich
            - Someone who lives a healthy lifestyle and is in a habit of exercising doesn't respond to " six easy steps to losing weight"
            - Everyone choose cheaper, quicker option over the better long-term solution
        - Peer pressure
            - It works not because the majority or the experts are always right, but because we fear that we may be wrong.
