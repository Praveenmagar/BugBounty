
# Location
## G + mouse: to move the object, then left click to post there and right click to bring back to initial position
## G + y + mouse: to move object in y direction
## G+ x + mouse: to move object in x direction
## G+ z + mouse: to move object in z direction
## After moving press right click to bring back in previous location and press left key to stay in change location

## To see all this press N, so that movement of location x,y and z as well as scaling all will be shown in right side

# Scaling
## To scale use : S + mouse
## To move in x axis: S + X + mouse
